Humans have reached Mars

The Starship rocket successfully landed on Mars

After a 10-month journey, the Starship rocket successfully landed on Mars, marking a major milestone for space exploration. The rocket, which is designed to carry humans to the Red Planet, landed in the Tharsis region of Mars, which is known for its volcanoes and canyons. The landing was the first successful landing of a Starship rocket on Mars, and it was a major milestone for space exploration. The rocket is expected to be used to carry humans to the Red Planet in the future.
